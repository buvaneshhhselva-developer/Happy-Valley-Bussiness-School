About Us

Building Leaders for a Dynamic World

Happy Valley Business School is a premier management institution in Coimbatore, dedicated to nurturing visionary leaders through rigorous academics and real-world exposure.

Est. 1999

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Located in the heart of Coimbatore, Happy Valley Business School has been at the forefront of management education for over two decades. Our institution blends traditional business wisdom with cutting-edge industry practices.

We are committed to developing ethical, innovative, and globally competent business leaders who drive positive change in organizations and society.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in management education, fostering innovation and ethical leadership.

To deliver transformative learning experiences through industry-aligned curriculum, research, and holistic student development.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions, programs, and campus life.

Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories). Valid scores in CAT, MAT, XAT, CMAT, or TANCET are required. Final-year students may also apply.

The admission process includes online application, entrance exam score submission, group discussion, personal interview, and document verification. Applications open in January and close in July each year.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, and special scholarships for women entrepreneurs and sports achievers. Education loan assistance is also provided through partner banks.

Separate hostel facilities for men and women are available on campus with furnished rooms, Wi-Fi, mess, laundry, and 24/7 security. Hostel seats are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.

Our placement cell offers resume workshops, aptitude training, mock interviews, GD practice, and connects students with 200+ recruiting companies. Summer internships are mandatory for all full-time programs.
